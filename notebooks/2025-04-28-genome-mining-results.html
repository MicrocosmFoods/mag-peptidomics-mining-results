Mining Bioactive Molecules from Fermented Food Bacterial Genomes

This report summarizes results from mining bacterial genome datasets sourced from various fermented foods, including ~200 isolates from the BacDive database and ~11,500 metagenome-assembled genomes (MAGs) from multiple metagenomic studies of fermented foods. We used the bacMAGmining Nextflow workflow to predict various molecule types from these genomes, including small ORF (smORF) peptides, cleavage peptides, and biosynthetic gene clusters.
